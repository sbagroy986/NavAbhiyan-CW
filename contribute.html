Contribute

We welcome all local residents, friends and believers in our cause to share their abundance with this really needy section of society.


We welcome all contributions in the following forms:

  • Time to teach the curriculum or various skills

  • Money to adopt a child and sponsor all their activities

  • Food to provide nutrition and an incentive to attend school

    Donations in kind, in the form of books, stationary, uniforms, clothes, toys and other such usable items

You can sponsor a child
For private school education - ₹15,000 a year
For morning school at Neev - ₹3,000 a year

We have a long way to go before we can consider our job done but with your support and the blessings of those innocent hearts that we work with we are growing from strength to strength.

The satisfaction from this selfless endeavour cannot be defined.The pleasure cannot be expressed. Our growth and our success is because of the small efforts of some kind people like you. Be it financial, moral or social, your help is invaluable in making sure that we are not alone in this Will of God. We are all together His instruments.


Please note

> All donations are exempt u/s 80G of the Income Act, 1961

> Financial Accounts and Expenditures of Nav-Abhiyaan are periodically audited as per the Statutory Government Norms
