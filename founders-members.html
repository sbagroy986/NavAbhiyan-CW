Team at NavAbhiyan

Our team is a small, but closely-knit, group of people. We have a mix of volunteers and founders working everyday.



Smt. Vinita Nagar

  • Founder

She is a post-graduate in social work and has served as Secretery, Delhi Social Welfare Board, Government of Delhi. She retired in 1994.
She then founded NavAbhiyan in 1995, in memory of her beloved mother. She has also been working as an advisor to the Unniti Foundation.
NavAbhiyan is working under her able leadership.


Dr. Veena Khanna

  • Executive Member

She has a Master of Arts degree and PhD. She retired from the Hindi department of Lakshmibai college, Delhi University and has been serving as a Secretary to the Unniti Foundation, India for 21 years. She is also a part of the All India Women's Council, Bhagwan Dass Road, New Delhi.
She has more than 21 years of exposure and affiliation with a number of NGOs working in the social sector for women empowerment and social upliftment. She has also served as the Executive Secretary of the CPWD Wives' Association for 5 years.
