NavAbhiyan

Nav-Abhiyan literally means a new movement to build bridges that break the shackles of the walls of caste, religion, sect, language, status & gender.

What is NavAbhiyan?


NavAbhiyan is the title we have given to our new movement.We are a non-governmental organization (NGO) working towards the welfare of the less fortunate. Through our programs, we are trying to reach some goals:

  • Enhancing the self-esteem of individuals by promoting self-reliance
  • Bridging across intra-social walls of caste,religion,sect,language,state,status & gender
  • To fulfill our social responsibilities towards the less fortunate for the socio-economic empowerment of all sections, for the overall welfare of humanity

Our Belief

NavAbhiyan was basically conceptualised with the idea of 'Nishkaam Karma'- the message of the Shrimad Bhagwat Geeta. We practice & promote the concepts.


"Selfless giving without expectation of anything in return"

This, in short, sums up the ethos of our social responsibility - as humans, towards humans.

It is with these sentiments that NavAbhiyan was conceived back in 1995 by our patron memebers and our committed and devoted team of voluntary workers. (Check Founders&Members page for more information.

Our ideas then were the same as now:

  • To give back to the society which has made us what we are today
  • To empower the grass-roots to make our country the superpower it can be
  • To unleash the humanity that lies within us, not through pity but through sympathy
  • To find within us the human-being, buried under the layers of class,creed,relegion,education,competition,economic & social contexts
