Navya



Navya is our vocational programme for the needy students of Lakshmibai college, Ashok Vihar to promote the self-reliance of the needy girls.

  • Block printing unit set-up with financial aid from Prerna and Bank of India and patronage of the college Principal and staff

  • Computer Training awailing services of the Som Dutt Mobile Computer Training Programme

  • On the job training programme.During summer vacations the trained girls started imparting block-printing training to various Sarvodaya Schools. 210 girls received training under this scheme and 5 were sent to AIWC, Bhagwan Das Road for an advanced course. Every year these girls exhibit and sell their creations at various fairs and the Blind School Diwali Mela.





We shifted the Vocational Training Centre from the Lakshmibai college to a hired Jhuggi. Young girls working as domestic help in and around the locality were keen to learn various crafts and arts to make their life interesting during their free time. We also hired a special instructor from the Jan Shikshan Sansthan.
